What is CARL?

CARL (short for "Coaching Analysis tool for Rocket League") is a free Rocket League analysis tool designed to help players understand the game in more depth. Whether you're a casual player, a competitive pro, a caster, a coach, an observer,.. CARL can provide valuable insights into your gameplay that can help you improve your skills.

With CARL, you can access advanced data and statistics that are not available within the game itself, such as player heatmaps, goalmaps, boost usage charts, and more. By analyzing this data, you can identify areas of your gameplay that need improvement and work on them to become a better player.

If you're a caster or analyst, CARL can be a powerful tool for real-time player performance tracking. With CARL, you can access detailed player and team stats, such as shot accuracy, boost usage, and ball touches, to provide insightful commentary and analysis during matches.
